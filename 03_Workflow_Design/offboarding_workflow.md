# Offboarding Workflow (HR SaaS–Mapped)

This document defines a structured, compliance-focused offboarding workflow designed using a SaaS product mindset. The workflow prioritizes audit readiness, access risk mitigation, and consistent execution across HR, managers, and IT.

---

## Workflow Objective

To ensure a controlled and compliant employee exit process that:
- Minimizes legal and access risks
- Ensures timely completion of notice period obligations
- Provides clear ownership and system traceability
- Supports HRIS-driven execution and reporting

---

## Workflow Overview

**Trigger → Action → Outcome**

Exit Initiated → Status Updated → Notice Period Tracked → Clearances Completed → Employee Record Closed

---

## Step 1: Exit Initiation

**Trigger Event**
- Employee resignation submission  
- OR management-approved termination decision

**Owner**
- Employee (resignation)
- HR / Management (termination)

**Inputs**
- Resignation letter or termination approval

**Outcome**
- Offboarding workflow initiated

---

## Step 2: Status Update & Communication

**HRIS Action**
- Update employee status to `Exit Initiated`

**Owner**
- HR

**Outputs**
- Exit timeline visible in system
- Stakeholders notified (employee, manager, payroll)

**Compliance Checkpoint**
- Notice period calculated as per labor requirements

---

## Step 3: Notice Period Tracking

**HRIS Action**
- Record notice period start and end dates

**Owners**
- HR
- Manager
- Employee

**Activities**
- Handover planning
- Attendance and obligation tracking

**Outcome**
- Notice period completion confirmed

---

## Step 4: Clearance & Access Revocation

**HRIS Action**
- Assign clearance checklist tasks

**Owners**
- HR
- Manager
- IT

**Clearance Areas**
- Asset handover
- System access revocation
- Knowledge transfer completion

**Decision Point**
- If all clearances completed → proceed  
- If pending items → hold final settlement

---

## Step 5: Final Settlement & Record Closure

**HRIS Action**
- Final settlement calculated and recorded
- Exit documents generated
- Employee record archived

**Owners**
- HR
- Payroll

**Outcome**
- Employee formally exited
- No active access or liabilities remain

---

## Success Criteria

- All compliance documentation completed
- Access fully revoked
- Exit settlement finalized and recorded
- Employee lifecycle accurately closed in HRIS

---

## SaaS Enablement Perspective

This workflow demonstrates how offboarding can be treated as a **risk-sensitive product flow**, where system status, approvals, and checkpoints prevent execution errors and compliance gaps.
