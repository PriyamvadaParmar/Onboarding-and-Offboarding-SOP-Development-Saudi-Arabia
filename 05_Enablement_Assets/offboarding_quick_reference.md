## Workflow Summary

**Trigger:** Resignation or termination approval

1. Initiate exit workflow
2. Update employee status
3. Track notice period
4. Complete clearances
5. Process final settlement
6. Close employee record

---

## Ownership Snapshot

| Step | Owner |
|-----|------|
| Exit initiation | Employee / HR |
| Status update | HR |
| Notice tracking | HR / Manager |
| Clearances | HR / IT / Manager |
| Final settlement | HR / Payroll |

---

## Key Compliance Rules

- Final settlement cannot proceed without full clearance
- System access must be revoked before record closure
- Notice period deviations require documented approval

---

## Common Errors to Avoid

- Closing records before access revocation
- Manual tracking outside HRIS
- Missing clearance approvals
