## Workflow Summary

**Trigger:** Offer accepted

1. Create employee record (Draft / Pending)
2. Collect mandatory documents
3. Verify and approve documents
4. Assign and record policy acknowledgements
5. Conduct first-day formalities
6. Activate employee status

---

## Ownership Snapshot

| Step | Owner |
|-----|------|
| Record creation | HR |
| Document submission | Employee |
| Verification | HR |
| Policy acknowledgement | Employee |
| Activation | HR |

---

## Key Compliance Rules

- Employee status must remain non-active until all mandatory documents are verified
- Policy acknowledgements are mandatory before activation
- Any rejected document must be re-uploaded and re-approved

---

## Common Errors to Avoid

- Activating employee status before document verification
- Tracking approvals outside the HRIS
- Missing acknowledgement timestamps
