# Employee Communication Templates – Onboarding

These templates standardize employee communication during onboarding to reduce confusion and improve experience.

---

## 1. Pre-Joining Email Template

**Subject:** Welcome! Next Steps for Your Joining

Hello {{Employee Name}},

Welcome to {{Company Name}}!

To ensure a smooth onboarding process, please complete the following before your joining date:

- Upload the required documents
- Review and acknowledge company policies

You will receive system notifications with instructions.

If you have any questions, feel free to reach out to HR.

We look forward to having you onboard.

Best regards,  
HR Team

---

## 2. Document Rework Request Template

**Subject:** Action Required: Document Update Needed

Hello {{Employee Name}},

We’ve reviewed your submitted documents and require a minor update before proceeding.

Please log into the HR system and re-upload the requested document at your earliest convenience.

Thank you for your cooperation.

Best regards,  
HR Team
