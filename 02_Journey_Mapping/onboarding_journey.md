# Onboarding Journey Mapping

This document maps the employee onboarding journey end-to-end, designed using a SaaS product workflow mindset. The goal is to ensure consistency, compliance, and clarity across HR, managers, and employees while remaining compatible with common HRIS platforms.

## Journey Objective
Enable a predictable, compliant, and low-friction onboarding experience that minimizes HR interpretation and supports system-driven execution.

---

## Stage 1: Pre-Joining

**Trigger:** Offer accepted via email or ATS confirmation

### Key Activities
- Offer acceptance recorded
- Employment contract issued
- Pre-joining communication sent to employee
- Initial document list shared

### Owners
- HR

### Inputs
- Signed offer letter
- Employee personal details

### Outputs
- Contract prepared
- Pre-joining checklist initiated

### Compliance Checkpoints
- Offer terms acknowledged
- Contract aligned with labor requirements

---

## Stage 2: Employee Record Initialization

### Key Activities
- Employee record created in HRIS (Draft / Pending)
- Role, department, and reporting manager assigned

### Owners
- HR

### Inputs
- Employee personal and job details

### Outputs
- Employee profile available for onboarding actions

### System Touchpoints
- HRIS employee module

---

## Stage 3: Document Collection & Verification

### Key Activities
- Mandatory documents collected (ID, visa, contract, etc.)
- Documents uploaded to HRIS
- Verification and approvals completed

### Owners
- Employee (submission)
- HR (verification)

### Inputs
- Uploaded documents

### Outputs
- Verified employee documentation

### Compliance Checkpoints
- Mandatory documents uploaded
- Approval status recorded

---

## Stage 4: First-Day Formalities

### Key Activities
- Orientation session
- Policy acknowledgements
- System access initiation (if applicable)

### Owners
- HR
- Manager

### Inputs
- Verified employee profile

### Outputs
- Employee formally onboarded

### System Touchpoints
- Policy acknowledgement tracking
- Status update

---

## Stage 5: Status Activation

### Key Activities
- Employee status updated to Active
- Onboarding marked as complete

### Owners
- HR

### Outputs
- Employee active in HRIS

### Success Criteria
- All compliance checkpoints completed
- Employee ready for productive work

---

## Journey Design Principles
- Clear triggers and ownership
- Embedded compliance checks
- HRIS-aware execution
- Reduced manual follow-ups
